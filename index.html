About us

  • Family fun center located in Lasalle, Quebec.
  • Affordable prices for children aged 3 - 11. We have a top of the line play structure.
  • We host birthday parties and special events that you and your children will never forget!
  • We also offer special availability upon reservation during the week for daycares and large groups.

The Benefits

  • FunZone encourages kids to reach the farthest reaches and climb the highest heights.
  • An hour in the family fun center, participating in a Zumba class, a gymnastics routine or a sports game!
  • Today's parents are looking for a safe, fun and affordable environment to take their families.In today's fast paced society and it is important to provide a family friendly destination that is fun in our community. FunZone answers that demand.
