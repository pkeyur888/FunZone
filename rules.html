Fun Zone Rules :-

  • Socks must be warn at ALL times
  • A parent MUST be present at all times ; FUNZONE is NOT a daycare
  • Upon entry the parent acknowledges that they are fully responsible for their children; FUNZONE is NOT responsible for any accidental injuries
  • Children under the age of 4 MUST be accompanied by an adult in the play area AT ALL TIMES
  • Safe play in the amusement facility is mandatory
  • Please be friendly with each other, violence will NOT be tolerated verbally or physically in anyway
  • To ensure safety there will be no; throwing of the balls, running outside the play area or aggressive behavior
  • No outside toys will be permitted in the play area
  • Outdoor food and drink is NOT allowed; the FUNZONE snack bar is always open
